About Bhavayami Nrithalaya

Welcome to Bhavayami Nrithalaya, Trivandrum's leading dance academy for children, students, and aspiring dancers of all ages.

Located in the heart of Thiruvananthapuram, we specialize in making dance accessible and enjoyable for kids and students. Our experienced instructors have successfully trained hundreds of children from beginner to advanced levels in classical and traditional dance forms.

We offer flexible schedules including weekend dance classes and after-school programs perfect for busy students. From your child's first dance steps to competition preparation and Arangetram, we provide personalized attention to help every student excel.

Bharatanatyam Classes for Kids

Classical Dance for Children & Students

Introduce your child to the ancient art of Bharatanatyam. Our beginner-friendly classes teach students traditional techniques, expressions, and storytelling. Perfect for children aged 5 and above, with weekend batches available.

  • Theory and practical sessions

Kerala Folk Dance for Children

Traditional Group Dance

Let your kids celebrate Kerala's rich culture through vibrant folk dances. Group classes perfect for school cultural programs, Onam celebrations, and annual day performances.

Thiruvathira Classes

Traditional Group Dance for Girls
