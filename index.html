About Bhavayami Nrithalaya

Welcome to Bhavayami Nrithalaya, Trivandrum's leading center for classical and traditional dance education.

Located in the heart of Thiruvananthapuram, we are dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Indian classical dance forms. Our experienced instructors bring decades of expertise in training students of all ages and skill levels.

Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps in dance or an advanced student preparing for competitions, we provide personalized attention and comprehensive training to help you excel.

Our Dance Programs

Discover the perfect dance form for you

Bharatanatyam

Classical Dance

Master the ancient art of Bharatanatyam with traditional techniques, expressions, and storytelling. Our structured curriculum covers from basic adavus to complex jathis and padams.

  • Traditional training methods
  • Arangetram preparation
  • Theory and practical sessions

Semi-Classical Dance

Fusion Dance

Experience the beautiful blend of classical techniques with contemporary expressions. Perfect for those who love versatility in dance forms.

  • Modern choreography
  • Cinematic dance styles
  • Expression training

Folk Dance

Traditional Kerala Dance

Celebrate Kerala's rich cultural heritage through vibrant folk dances. Learn the traditional movements and rhythms passed down through generations.

  • Various folk styles
  • Cultural immersion
  • Group performances

Thiruvathira

Kerala Traditional Dance

Learn the graceful Thiruvathira, a traditional women's dance of Kerala performed in circular formations with synchronized movements.

  • Festival performances
  • Group coordination
  • Traditional songs

Nrithyayoga

Dance & Wellness

Experience the unique fusion of yoga and dance, promoting physical fitness, mental well-being, and artistic expression in harmony.

  • Fitness through dance
  • Mindfulness practices
  • Flexibility training

Competition Training

Specialized Coaching

Intensive training for dance competitions at school, state, and national levels. Personalized choreography and performance enhancement.

  • Custom choreography
  • Performance coaching
  • Stage presence training
